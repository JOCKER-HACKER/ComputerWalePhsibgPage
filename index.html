About us

Vidya Bharati Institute of Culture Education was established in Kurukshetra (Haryana) on July 30, 1984 and formally was registered on September 4, 1984 under section XXI of Society Registration Act 1860, whose registration number is 320. - Under the Section 9 (4) of the Haryana Society Registration Act, 2012, it was renewed on 29 June 2016, whose registration number is HR 579201601170.
